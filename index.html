.rocthinc

from chat to chapter

rocthinc formats conversation into clear, structured documents—fast and stateless. Share a chat, and rocthinc returns a polished Markdown file without storing your data. Simple. Organized. You keep the file.

  • Reads multi-speaker chats and preserves role structure.
  • Exports crisp Markdown (and HTML) with a hash for integrity—no server-side retention.
  • Lightweight REST API: POST /export
Open the app →
Note: the app may be private during setup; the link remains constant.
how it works
  1. Send or share a chat transcript with rocthinc.
  2. It parses, cleans, and formats each message.
  3. It hashes your export and returns the file—then discards the input.
sample request
curl -X POST https://rocthinc.up.railway.app/export \ -H "Content-Type: application/json" \ -d '{"messages":[{"speaker":"user","content":"Save this"},{"speaker":"assistant","content":"Done."}]}' \ --output export.md
the name

rocthinc — Born of silicon, shaped to format your words and return them beautifully—no memory, no storage, just a little bit of thinking.

privacy

Stateless by design. rocthinc processes the content you submit for each individual request as an input, then outputs that content as a formatted document for you to save, and discards the data. No analytics, no background retention.
